Job List

Cashier

A cashier’s job is to stand at the counter and take customers’ orders. This is hands down the most boring job, but this position is necessary to keep the other jobs running.

Choose this job

Expectations:

  • Take customers’ orders quickly and correctly

  • No one wants to be a cashier, so the manager will do everything within their power to keep you working the till

Pros:

  • Easy to play

  • The manager may give you a bonus check for taking one for the team

Cons:

  • This job sucks

  • No one will willingly take over for you so you may be trapped as a cashier indefinitely

Cook

Cooks are in charge of baking the pizzas and sending the finished orders to the pizza boxers. This job can be hectic depending on how many cashiers are working, but it is the most fun in my opinion.

Choose this job

Expectations:

  • Prepare and cook the pizzas efficiently

  • Usually, there is only one cashier working so it is easy to keep up with orders

  • If there are a lot of orders, it is fairly easy to catch up by preparing many pizzas at once (you can quickly cook plain cheese pizzas and add the toppings afterwards)

Pros:

  • One of the more engaging jobs

  • The manager is more likely to reward you for a job well done because they often check the kitchen

Cons:

  • Directly relies on a cashier to be working

  • Pizzas can go stale if they are out too long, so watch how much you prep

Pizza Boxer

Pizza boxers prepare customer orders for delivery, by boxing pizzas and sending finished orders to the delivery table. This job is extremely simple, but surprisingly entertaining.

Choose this job

Expectations:

  • Box pizzas quickly so the conveyor isn't held up

  • Accurately place the finished orders on their respective conveyor belts to efficiently deposit orders

Pros:

  • Easy to play

  • The boxes make a nice sound

Cons:

  • It is easy to accidentally close the boxes before the pizza is inserted, decreasing your worker rating

  • The conveyor often jams on its own, and the cook might assume it is because you aren't keeping up with the orders

Delivery

Delivery drivers pick up the finished orders and deliver them to their respective houses. There are only three streets but it can still get confusing keeping track of orders.

Choose this job

Expectations:

  • Control your delivery vehicle well to quickly deliver pizzas

  • Give houses their correct order

  • Keep track of which houses ordered so you don't have to backtrack

Pros:

  • Driving a car may be more exciting to you than standing in the pizzeria

Cons:

  • This job is repetitive due to having to constantly view the delivery cutscene

  • Controlling the car may be difficult for new players

Supplier

A supplier's responibility is to keep track of the stock in the pizzeria, and deliver supplies as needed. This is my second favourite job because of how engaging it is.

Choose this job

Expectations:

  • Keep track of stock and load the supply truck accordingly

  • Drive the truck back and forth between the supply depot and pizzeria

  • Keep in mind that you will need to back the truck into the delivery zone

Pros:

  • As long as you are keeping on track of stock, you do not need to constantly supply because the maximum stock is 99

Cons:

  • Sometimes people steal your truck, leaving you to walk across the map to get a new vehicle

  • The supply trucks drive slowly

Manager

The manager's job is to make sure employees are working. They can reward or punish players based on how well they are doing their jobs. I personally think the other jobs are more fun, however.

Choose this job

Expectations:

  • Make sure each position has at least one worker who is doing their job

  • Don't take this job too seriously, I mean it's Roblox Pizza Place

  • Abuse your power, but be nice to workers if you want them to do their jobs

Pros:

  • You get to sit in a cool office

  • You can put players back to work if they are slacking

Cons:

  • This position is not actually that powerful because banning a player requires a vote kick

  • It is difficult managing a bunch of small children
