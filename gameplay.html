Starting Off

Basic Controls:

  • move using the WASD keys, jump using the space bar
  • Pan the camera around by holding down the right mouse button, zoom in and out using the scroll wheel
  • Interact with objects using either the left mouse button or the ‘E’ key. Objects that require the ‘E’ key are usually marked as such

Did you skip the tutorial? The1stCEO will help you through these trying times.

The first thing you will be asked to do when you start Work at a Pizza Place is to choose a job. This can be changed anytime by going to another job's work station. Please refer to the Jobs page if you are having trouble deciding.

Job Mechanics


Cashier

  • Interact with customers using ‘E’.
  • Choose a response in the bottom left corner of the screen (it doesn’t matter which one).
  • Click on the correct item the customer ordered.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Do not choose this job.
  • You will be stuck as a cashier for a long period of time because no one is going to take over for you.
  • The manager might give you a bonus check for taking one for the team, but at what cost?
  • If someone is actually doing their job as a cashier, AFK next to them to give them the false hope that they will be freed from this hell.
  • This is a cry for help.

Cook

  • Check the order board, if the item is soda, go to step 4, if it is pizza, proceed to step 2.
  • Make the corresponding pizza by clicking and dragging the dough onto the table, followed by the sauce, cheese, and topping if required.
  • Put the pizza in an oven and wait until the timer is done.
  • Drag the finished order onto the conveyor belt.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Lay out multiple doughs across the table to efficiently prepare the pizzas.
  • If you start a fire, there is a fire extinguisher along the window-side wall.
  • If you burn a pizza or if it goes stale, there is a disposal next to the fire extinguisher.
  • If you get bored, start a fire.
  • If the manager scolds you for "making a mess" use your 7 other throwaway accounts to vote kick them from their position.

Pizza Boxer

  • Drag the pizza boxes from the wall onto the table.
  • Drag the incoming pizzas into the open boxes and click to close them.
  • Drag the finished pizza boxes or sodas onto their respective conveyor belts.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Lay out multiple boxes across the table to efficiently box the pizzas.
  • If you accidentally close a box, you can quickly reopen it by dragging the lid open. If you do not reopen the box in time, it will disappear, but don't worry as this does not affect the box supply.

Delivery

  • Click on the finished orders on the table to pick them up. Grab as many as you can
  • Go outside and get in a delivery car by pressing 'E' next to one. You can exit the car by jumping.
  • Drive to the orders' respective house. There are only three streets: A, B, and C; which have up to 6 houses on them.
  • Hold the respective house's order and walk up to the door to deliver. If a house ordered multiple items, click on the additional items during the cutscene.

Tips and Tricks:

  • Click on the order you are delivering as you are driving, so you don't have to bring it out as you are walking to the door.
  • You can move your character back to your car during the delivery cutscene. When the cutscene finishes, you will automatically be in the car.
  • If you are struggling with the driving controls go get a driver's license IRL, so you can master driving in Work at a Pizza Place.

Supplier

  • Check the supply of each item in the pizzeria. The maximum amount is 99.
  • If an item is running low, step on the button that corresponds to the item's colour.
  • Drag the supply boxes that fall out of the chute into a supply truck. Fill up the truck as much as possible.
  • Drive the truck back to the pizzeria, and back it into the delivery zone. Pull the lever on the side of the truck to deposit the items on the conveyor. Make sure you retract it after so you can refill the truck.

Tips and Tricks:

  • You can drop boxes into your truck without getting out.
  • Neatly stack the boxes in the truck to deliver a lot at a time.
  • Turn the truck without moving forwards or backwards to easily back it into the delivery station.
  • Play Jenga with the supply boxes to pass time if you are waiting for someone else to return with the truck.

Manager

  • Sit at the manager's desk if the position is open to become the new manager.
  • Press 'E' next to a player to open a list of actions you can do for them as manager.
  • Abuse your power.

Tips and Tricks:

  • If a player has 0 stars as a worker, you can fire them.
  • Despite there being a 'remove manager' vote kick system, it is very unlikely that enough people will vote to have you removed. Do with that information what you will.
